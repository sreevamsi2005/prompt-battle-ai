# Video Assets

Place your booth MP4 files here. The demo ships with placeholder clips.

## Required files

- `samurai.mp4` — challenge video 1
- `cyberpunk.mp4` — challenge video 2
- `ocean.mp4` — challenge video 3
- `recreation-low.mp4` — shown when score &lt; 40
- `recreation-medium.mp4` — score 40–75
- `recreation-high.mp4` — score &gt; 75

Edit `data/challenges.json` to match your filenames and hidden prompts.
